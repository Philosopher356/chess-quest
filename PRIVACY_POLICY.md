# Privacy Policy for Chess Quest

**Last updated: April 16, 2026**

## Overview

Chess Quest is a chess learning app designed for children ages 5 and older. We take your privacy seriously, especially when it comes to children's data.

## Data Collection

**Chess Quest does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** All app data is stored locally on your device.

### What is stored locally:
- Player profiles (name, avatar selection, age)
- Learning progress (completed lessons, stars earned)
- Game history (games played against the AI)
- Puzzle statistics (puzzles attempted, rating)
- Settings preferences (sound, notifications)

### What is NOT collected:
- No personal information is sent to any server
- No analytics or tracking data
- No location data
- No photos, contacts, or device identifiers
- No advertising data

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Chess Quest is designed for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):
- We do not collect any personal information from children
- We do not require account creation or login
- We do not contain any third-party advertising
- We do not contain any in-app purchases
- All data remains on the device

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted.

## Third-Party Services

Chess Quest does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising SDKs.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
